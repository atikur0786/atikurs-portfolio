// About Me

Hello! I'm Atikur Satter Mondal, a full-stack developer with over three years of experience crafting secure, scalable, and performance-driven web applications.

At Jukshio Technology Innovation, I’ve led the development of high-impact projects — including a real-time Video KYC platform, AI/ML data curation dashboards, and enterprise-grade internal tools — used by hundreds daily. I specialize in modern web technologies like Angular, React, Node.js, and PostgreSQL, and I love solving complex problems with clean, maintainable code.

My approach combines a deep understanding of frontend logic and backend architecture, along with experience in performance tuning, WebSockets, cloud migration, and API security. Whether it's building dynamic SPAs, streamlining user experiences, or architecting backend systems, I bring energy and ownership to everything I build.

I'm always learning, always building, and always excited to explore the next big thing in tech. When I'm not coding, you'll probably find me diving into open-source projects, sharpening my problem-solving skills, or just exploring the ever-evolving dev world.
